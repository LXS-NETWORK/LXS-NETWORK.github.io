How the launchpad works

Launching is three inputs and one button: a name, a ticker, and an optional image. There's nothing to deploy and no liquidity pool to fund — the chain handles the hard part. The second you hit create, your token exists as a real on-chain smart contract and is tradeable immediately.

Price is set by a bonding curve, not a market maker you have to seed: as more people buy, the price moves up the curve; when they sell, it moves back down. Early buyers are early by definition. Everyone — including you, the creator — buys on the same curve at the same price the moment it opens. No presale, no hidden allocation, no team bag waiting to dump on the people who show up.

Same curve for everyone. You and every buyer trade on the same bonding curve from the second the coin opens — no presale, no hidden allocation, no team bag.

Your coin. Your community. Your curve.

A token on LXS isn't rented space on someone else's platform — it lives on the blockchain, replicated across every node, and stays alive even if this website disappears. It's yours to rally people around.
