What is LXS?

LXS is a proof-of-work Layer-1 blockchain — its own independent network, like Bitcoin, not a token living on someone else's chain. New coins come out of mining only, one block at a time, so there's no hidden tap of insider coins and no company minting supply and handing it to friends.

Think of it the way Bitcoin started: a level playing field where the coin is earned with real work, not printed. On top of that base, LXS adds one thing Bitcoin never did — a one-click launchpad, so any community can spin up its own token and trade it instantly. This website is just a window into the chain; the network keeps running whether this page is up or not.

Fair from block one

Most of crypto is gatekept: presales for insiders, a premine for the team, a VC round before you ever heard of it. LXS launched the opposite way — the way you can join it today.

  • No presale. No ICO. No VC round. No whitelist. There was no fastest-fingers token sale and no early-access tier.
  • Mined from the very first block. From genesis, anyone with a computer could mine LXS. Every coin beyond the founder allocation is earned with real work.
  • The founder allocation is disclosed, not hidden. 20,000,000 LXS were created at genesis for the founder — that's it. It's written into the genesis file that ships with every miner, and it becomes a smaller and smaller share of supply as ~100M more is mined over roughly 500 years.
We won't pretend to be premine-free — we'll be honest about it. The founder holding is small, fixed, and visible on-chain. Everything else is mined, in the open, by whoever shows up. That's the whole pitch: a level field and a launch button, not a promise of profit.

Get started in three steps

  1. Get a wallet. Open the LXS wallet — it runs in your browser, keeps your key on your device, and needs no account or email. Or add LXS to MetaMask.
  2. Get some LXS. The fastest way is to mine it — free, on a normal computer. A brand-new wallet can also grab a little free gas from the faucet to make its first move.
  3. Use it. Send LXS to anyone, or launch your own token and share it with your community.

Launch a token in one click

Every community can have its own coin on LXS. Pick a name, a ticker, and an image — that's it. Your token is a real on-chain smart contract, instantly tradeable on a bonding curve, with no code to write and no upfront liquidity to seed.

The price is set by the curve, not by a pool you have to fund: buy early and you're literally early on the curve. Every launch plays by the same rules — same rules for the founder and the newest wallet.

Run the network. It's yours.

LXS lives because of the people who run it. There's no application and no gatekeeper — just start.

  • Mine — secure the chain and earn LXS. One command, or one click.
  • Run a node — become part of the network so it can never be switched off from one place.
  • Build — launch a token, read the docs, or dig into the open-source code.
  • Spread the word — the whole thing grows by adoption, one real person at a time.
