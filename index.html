What is LXS?

LXS is a proof-of-work layer-1 blockchain. That means there's no company minting coins and handing them to friends — the coins come out of mining, one block at a time, and anyone with a computer can do it.

On top of the chain, anyone can launch a token with their community. Tokens are real smart contracts living on the blockchain across every node, not entries in some website's database. This website is just a window into the chain — the network keeps running whether this page is up or not.

Where does the value come from? From people actually using it — mining it, launching on it, trading on it. Nothing here promises a price or a return, and we won't pretend otherwise. LXS is worth whatever the people who show up decide to make it worth.

LXS is an open, mined, community-run blockchain. Your keys never leave your wallet. Value comes from adoption, not from promises — we make no claims about price or returns.
